Two hands, one hook, a hundred hanks

HANKED started with two Computer Science students who were supposed to be debugging code but somehow ended up untangling yarn instead.

how it started

From classmates to co-founders

We were just classmates until our third year, when assignments, deadlines, and university chaos turned us into friends. Somewhere between "just one crochet project" and "we should totally sell these," HANKED was born.

how we work

A creative escape, stitched into a passion

What began as a creative escape from screens and coding errors became a shared passion for handmade creations. Today, we're friends, co-founders, crochet enthusiasts, and professional buyers of yarn we definitely don't need.

Welcome to HANKED — where friendship, creativity, and a little bit of chaos are woven into every stitch.
